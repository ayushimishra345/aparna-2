Pan Pan ki taraf se

Do Lafzon Mein, Aapke Liye

kuch shers, sirf aapke naam — dil ke seedhe seedhe

Aap hansti hain to lagta hai mausam badal gaya, duniya ke gham bhi aapke saamne machal gaya. Khamoshi mein bhi aap kuch kehti hain mujhse, ye dosti nahi, ek sukoon hai jo dil mein pal gaya.

Army brat hain aap, dil se bhi itni hi dilair, zindagi ke har morche par khadi rahi hain aap hazir. Naye naye shehron mein ghar basaati rahi hain aap, par apna dil kabhi nahi kiya kabhi fakir.

Sanki avocado kehte hain aapko yaaron mein, par maine dekha hai aapko sachchi wali naaron mein. Bahar ki khadaas, andar ki meethaas yahi toh hain aap, ek pyaar bhari roohi, chhupi in khamoosh baaron mein.

Aap deewar ban ke khadi hain apne dard ke aage, par hum hain jo us deewar ke peeche bhi aapko dekhte hain. Darr mat, ab aap akeli nahi ladengi, hum bhi aapke har toofan mein aapke saath chalte hain.

Moody billi aur sanki avocado ka yeh pyara rishta, koi samjhe na samjhe, hamein hai yeh saaf dikhta. Ladti hain, jhagadti hain, roothi rehti hain — par is dosti mein hai ek aaisa sukoon jo kabhi nahi bikhta.

Janamdin mubarak ho meri sabse pyaari dost, aap hi toh hain meri hasi ka ek sada behta srot. Khoob muskuraiye, khoob khiliye, khoob jee leejiye

Zindagi ne aapko diya bahut kuch sahna, naye ghar, nayi school, naya sab kuch mahna. Par aap har baar uth ke chali naye maidaan mein yahi aapki asli pehchaan hai, isi par aapko rehna.

Pan Pan ki duaon mein aap hamesha shaamil hain, aapke har sapne mein aapki taraf ek raah kaamil hai. Jab bhi ghabraiye, jab bhi akeli lage yeh duniya yaad rakhiyega, aapka dost ka dil aapke liye hamesha khaamil hai.

Happy Birthday Army Brat

Aapki zindagi ke baare mein sochta hoon toh ek baat jo sabse pehle dimag mein aati hai aap kitni strong hain. Army family mein palna, baar baar naye shehr, nayi jagah, nayi school, nayi dosti sab kuch baar baar shuru karna, fir se adjust karna, fir se roots banana. Ye sab koi aasaan nahi hota. Jis umar mein bachon ko sirf khelna aata hai, aap toh sikh chuki thi ki kaise apna saaman bina rulaaye pack karte hain, kaise naye chehron ke beech apna chehra dhoondhte hain. Par aapne yeh itni khoobsurti se kiya hai , meri Army Brat. Yahi aapki sabse badi pehchaan hai Jahan bhi jaati hain, apne sath ek ghar le jaati hain woh ghar jo aapke dil ke andar hai.

Main aaj phir se wahi din yaad kar raha hoon cricket wala ground, phir mess ki pehli baatein, aur phir college ka woh pehla hug😘.

Log aapko upar-upar se dekhte hain, Aparna ji. Aapki natural si beauty, aapki woh pyaari smile jo sab ko deewana karti hai. Unhe lagta hai aap "chill" hain, "thodi buddhu" hain, kisi baat ka khaas asar nahi hota. Kitne galat hain woh sab. Main jaanta hoon us smile ke peeche ek aisi insan hain jo andar se bahut zyada feel karta hain, bahut zyada sochta hain, bahut zyada dard uthata hain. Raat ko jab sab so jaate hain, tab shayad aap apne hi khayalon se ladta hain, akele. Par subah hote hi, hamesha chupaata hain. Hamesha doosron ke liye hans deta hain, jaise aapka dard kabhi exist hi nahi karta. Yeh aapka sabse pyaara hissa bhi hai par mein yhe jaanti hoon ki aap akele kitna jhel lete ho, aur kabhi kehte nahi.

Zindagi ne aapko bahut jaldi hi bahut kuch sikhaya. Ragging ka woh dravna phase, padhai ka stress, log aaye aur chale gaye aapne akele itna saha hai ki sochke hi mera dil bhaari hota hai. Mujhe yaad hai jab aapne pehli baar mujhe apne us phase ke baare mein bataya tha, Jab aap kehti hain "ab farq nahi padta," hum jante hai ki yeh aapka attitude nahi yeh ek deewar hai jo aapne khud ko bachane ke liye apne dil ke chaaron taraf kheench li hai, taaki dubara koi aapko utna na tod sake jitna pehle log tod gaye. Aur hum samjhte hai. Par aaj aapke birthday par, hum woh deewar nahi todna chahte hum bas aapko ye batana chahte hoon ki iske is taraf hum hamesha the, hain, aur rahenge. Aap chahe deewar rakhein ya na rakhein, hum kahin nahi jaayenge.

Aap aur Samridhi yaar, yeh aap dono ka sanki avocado aur moody billi wala bond kitna special hai! Dono ek-doosre ko thodna chahti hain, dono kabhi seedha pyaar nahi dikhayengi, dono bhot moody par ek-doosre ke bina dono ko chain nahi. Jab kabhi aap dono ka muh fula hota hai, poora kamra jaanta hai, aur phir ghanton baad woh hi dono sath baithi hoti hain jaise kuch hua hi nahi tha. Yeh jo aap dono ki hate love dosti hai na, isse hum sab ka hostel zinda tha. Khoob ladti rahiye, khoob roothiye, par kabhi saath mat chhodna — kyunki hum sab jaante hain ki bina ek-doosre ke aap dono adhuri lagti hain.

Aur woh yaadein Activa se Kandoli ki woh ride jismein haath zyada hurt hue par smile zyada aayi. Strawberry farm ki woh wanderings, jahan hum bacchon jaise has rahe the.Kunjapurni Mata Mandir ki woh subah jab andar se kuch settle ho gaya, jaise duniya ki sari uljhanein wahin reh gayi ho. Mussoorie ki woh vaadiyan jahan laga sab theek hai duniya mein, jahan har takleef chhoti lagne lagi. Dehradun ka McDonald's jahan ghanton ki pagal panti , aur kisi ko ghar jaane ki jaldi nahi thi. Maine in jagahon par sirf ghoomna nahi seekha — maine aapke saath zindagi ko mehsoos karna seekha. Jeena seekha.

Aaj aapke birthday par, aapki Pan Pan sirf ek cheez maangna chahti hai khush rahiye. Woh khushi jo pure dil se aaye, jo kisi ke jaane ya kisi ke aane se na badle. Woh smile jo sirf doosron ke liye nahi, apne liye bhi ho. Aap bahut deserve karti hain — pyaar, sukoon, aur woh sab kuch jo aap hamesha sabko dete aaye ho. Ab aapki baari hai lene ki, sirf dene ki nahi. Apne liye bhi thoda jiya kariye .

I Wish ki aap ki zindagi khushiyon bhari rahe .

Happy Birthday once again, sabse pyaari pure soul, hamari Army Brat, hamari Sanki Avocado. Aapka yeh saal pyaar se, sukoon se, aur un sab khushiyon se bhara rahe jo aap doosron ko dene mein kabhi kanjoosi nahi karti. I love you to the moon, the stars, and all the way back, and then some more. 🌸❤️
